Bio

I'm a student at Brown Univesrity interested in housing, science communication and health care.

My reporting focuses on housing and the economy. I'm curently a reporting intern for the Wall Street Journal's real estate bureau.

Previously, I worked at WNYC and Gothamist for New York Public Radio and the National Institute on Minority Health and Health Disparities at the NIH.

At Brown, I'm a research assistant at the School of Public Health and editor-in-chief of The Brown Daily Herald.
